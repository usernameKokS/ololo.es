What is the main purpose of MNFST?

We created MNFST to give every user an opportunity to capitalize on their social media profiles – one of the most valuable and important personal assets in the digital age.

Not so long ago, only celebrities and power bloggers with a massive following were able to monetize their social media presence. Later, micro-influencers came into play (top local bloggers and opinion leaders in smaller communities). However, smaller influencers still need a large enough audience to compete with traditional advertising channels.

Now, MNFST has finally introduced a completely open, transparent and fair platform that provides everyone, regardless of the size of their following, with an opportunity to promote and recommend the things they care about. In turn, advertisers reward users directly.
